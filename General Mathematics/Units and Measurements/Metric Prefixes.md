The metric prefixes increases the exponent by one until it reaches *kilo-* or *milli* where the succeeding prefixes are multiples of three.

| Prefix | Symbol | Exponent | Scientific Notation | Standard Notation |
| ------ | ------ | -------- | ------------------- | ----------------- |
| kilo-  | k      | 3        | $*10^3$             | 1000              |
| hecto- | h      | 2        | $*10^2$             | 100               |
| deca-  | da     | 1        | $*10^1$             | 10                |
| *none* |        |          | $*10^0$             | 1                 |
| deci-  | d      | -1       | $*10^{-1}$          | 0.1               |
| centi- | c      | -2       | $*10^{-2}$          | 0.01              |
| milli  | m      | -3       | $*10^{-3}$          | 0.001             |
