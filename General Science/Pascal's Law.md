Pascal's Law states that for an incompressible fluid in a enclosed space, the force exerted to it will transfer elsewhere to maintain pressure.
$$
F_{in} = F_{out}
$$
Since it is stated earlier that it is a consequence of maintaining pressure
$$
p_1=p_2
$$
And pressure can be defined as $p=\frac{F}{A}$, this formula can be used for when the area of two pistons vary.
$$
\frac{F_1}{A_1}=\frac{F_2}{A_2}
$$
